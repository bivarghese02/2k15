about us

Born in the campus of NIT-Calicut, Tathva has evolved to become one of the largest techno-management symposiums in India over the past twelve years. True to its motto, Tathva stands to usher the future society into an age of development brought over by innovation in technology. Over the years, Tathva has made its prime importance to cater to the public through exhibitions and world class lectures put together throughout the festival. Tathva also brings the world of innovation to our country through renowned scientists and luminaries who inspire the budding youth through their experiences and cutting edge research.


Tathva aims to encompass the entirety of our country’s current development plan by taking heed of our societal duties to use technology as a tool to help the address the significant problems many of our peers still face. Through the social initiatives, Tathva aims to construct a platform for the youth to empower sections of our community through eco-friendly and sustainable ventures. Tathva is foremost an organization run by the entire student community of NIT-C which is divided into three major committees for optimal operation. The organizational structure is headed by the Technical Affairs Secretary and features over 1000 coordinators to put together the spectacle called Tathva.Overall, Tathva incorporates all these distinct traits to become a role-model for the younger generation.

tathva 14

The last iteration of Tathva raised the standards of the symposium to a whole new level. Tathva 13’ achieved its success through a combination of technology and entertainment. The underlying spirit of the festival was realized through world class lectures by the likes of Jeff Lieberman and stunning exhibitions from the Indian Navy and ISRO amongst many. A due amount of technological entertainment was derived from the ever successful automobile exhibition ‘Wheels’ which stunned the masses through the sheer brilliance of the vehicles on display. Tathva 13’ evolved into one of the country’s leading platforms for innovation through its initiative Aavishkar, which undertook the technological development of a rural village in Kerala through eco-friendly philosophies. By retaining its individual zest to contribute to our nation’s progress, Tathva 13’ was the most inspiring edition of the annual festival yet.
